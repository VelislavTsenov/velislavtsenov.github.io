Florence is announced as a modern chypre-ambery scent with citruses, tangy-fruity notes and floral tones. Fruits and citruses open the composition symbolizing life and energy, and continue in the heart blending with a "radiant" flower bouquet and an "exotic" touch of hibiscus seed. The base introduces a "luxurious chypre" signature with a combination of patchouli, amber and musk. The perfume is developed by Marie Salamagne.
